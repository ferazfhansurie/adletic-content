FORMAT: 1080x1080px (1:1 square ratio — optimized for Facebook/Instagram feed ads), mobile-first.

LOGO (MUST INCLUDE — PROMINENT, EXACT MATCH):
The Adletic Agency logo is a circular badge/emblem style logo with these exact elements:
- A circular border in dark navy (#1B2A4A) with a light cream/off-white (#F5F0E8) background inside
- "ADLETIC" arched along the top inside the circle in bold, uppercase dark navy serif/slab-serif lettering with a sporty vintage feel
- "AGENCY" along the bottom of the circle in bold uppercase orange (#E86A2E) lettering
- CENTER ILLUSTRATION: A dynamic running man character in mid-sprint, facing right, holding a megaphone in his hand shouting forward. He wears an orange (#E86A2E) tank top/jersey, dark navy shorts, orange sneakers, and a dark navy cap. The style is clean line-art illustration with minimal shading — sporty, energetic, and playful
- Accent elements inside the circle: small golden/orange four-pointed stars scattered around the runner, a yellow lightning bolt near the megaphone, and curved orange/golden accent lines flanking the sides
- The overall logo style is vintage athletic badge meets modern agency — like a sports team crest but for a marketing agency
Place the logo prominently at the top-center of the poster, sized at roughly 25% of the poster width. On dark backgrounds, the circular badge retains its cream interior background.

SQUARE LAYOUT — TOP TO BOTTOM (1:1 canvas — compact and punchy):

ZONE 1 — TOP BAR (0-12% of height):
- Adletic Agency circular badge logo on the LEFT (sized ~12% of poster width)
- On the RIGHT: "adleticagency.com" in small dark navy text
- Thin orange-to-gold gradient line below spanning full width

ZONE 2 — HEADLINE + HERO (12-60% of height — merged for impact on square format):

- The word "Burning" rendered in vibrant orange (#E86A2E) with small illustrated flame accents — pops against the white background
- RIGHT HALF: Hero illustration in the SAME style as the logo (clean line-art, flat colors, dynamic energy) — a dramatic funnel/vortex with stylized Ringgit bills being pulled in and transforming into upward-shooting orange and gold arrows, bar charts, and lightning bolts erupting out the top
- Small illustrated megaphone icons and speed lines tying the visual to the brand
- This split layout (text left, illustration right) creates visual balance on the square canvas

ZONE 3 — STATS (60-75% of height):
- Three rounded pill badges in a horizontal row, evenly spaced across the full width, each with light gray (#F0F0F0) background, thin navy border, and bold navy text:
  * "12x ROAS" with a small orange upward arrow icon
  * "RM6.90/Lead" with a small orange target/bullseye icon
  * "98% Retention" with a small orange checkmark icon
- Small golden star accents between the badges

ZONE 4 — CTA + FOOTER (75-100% of height):
- Bold rounded rectangle CTA button, centered, with vibrant orange (#E86A2E) solid fill
- White bold uppercase text: "3-Week Trial — RM350"
- Small lightning bolt icons flanking the button text (matching logo style)
- Below button: a bonus badge/tag in a small rounded pill — dark navy background with white text: "+ FREE 15-sec AI Video Ad (worth RM350)" — this adds perceived value and makes the offer irresistible

- Bottom edge: thin dark navy (#1B2A4A) strip with WhatsApp green icon and "+60 112 167 672" in small white text — anchors the composition

BACKGROUND & VISUAL DESIGN:
- Clean white (#FFFFFF) background — bright, airy, and modern
- Very subtle light gray geometric pattern or dot grid at ultra-low opacity (5-10%) for texture — prevents the white from feeling flat
- Scattered small golden (#D4A017) four-pointed stars and orange lightning bolt accents throughout the background at low opacity — matching the logo's decorative elements
- Curved orange (#E86A2E) and golden (#D4A017) swoosh/accent lines flowing diagonally across the background — echoing the logo's flanking accent curves, but rendered at lighter opacity so they don't overpower the white
- Thin orange border line running along the left and right edges

- The white background makes the orange, navy, and gold elements POP with maximum contrast

- Sub-text: Light italic sans-serif — subtle

- White background with navy + orange + gold accents — clean, bright, and premium. NOT dark/moody

- Keep the floating dashboard UI cards but render them in a clean flat-design style with light gray backgrounds and navy/orange accents — matching the logo's warm palette

WHAT TO AVOID:
- No dark/moody backgrounds — the background MUST be white
- No stock photos, no generic handshake images, no smiling business people
- No hyper-realistic 3D renders — keep illustration style consistent with the logo's clean line-art
- No more than 20% text coverage on the image
- No cluttered layout — generous breathing space around each element
- No cheap gradients or generic drop shadows
- Do NOT change the color palette away from the logo's navy/orange/gold/cream scheme
