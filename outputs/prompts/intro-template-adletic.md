# Adletic Agency — 10s Intro Animation Prompt Template

## Reference Images
- Runner mascot (standalone character on white background)
- Full circular Adletic Agency logo with megaphone runner

---

## Prompt 1 — SAFE (img2vid from runner mascot)

White background. The illustrated runner character sprints directly toward camera with explosive energy, arms pumping, feet pounding. FPV crash zoom into the runner's chest as motion lines streak behind him. At the 5-second mark, the runner leaps forward — smash cut to the full Adletic Agency circular logo slamming into frame with a shockwave ripple effect. Logo holds center frame, slight breathing scale pulse. Orange and navy blue color palette. Bold graphic energy. Fast, aggressive, punchy motion throughout. Shot on 35mm lens, high shutter speed freeze-frame feel. No CGI look.

## Prompt 2 — CREATIVE (img2vid from logo)

White background, camera starts tight on empty space. Suddenly — the Adletic Agency runner mascot bursts through from the left in a full sprint, whip pan tracking him at high speed. He raises the megaphone — sound wave ripples explode outward in orange and navy. The ripples form into the circular Adletic Agency logo as the camera snap-zooms out to reveal the full emblem. Lightning bolt sparks flash. Stars shimmer briefly. Logo locks in center frame with a confident hold. Fast-paced, high-energy, graphic motion style. Orange and dark navy palette. Clean white background throughout. No CGI look.

## Prompt 3 — EXPERIMENTAL (text2vid, no reference needed)

Clean white void. A single orange running shoe hits the ground — impact sends navy ink splashes radiating outward, low-angle power shot looking up. Rapid barrel roll transition — the ink splashes morph mid-air into the silhouette of a sprinting runner holding a megaphone. FPV drone chase follows the runner from behind as speed lines streak past camera. Runner jumps — freeze frame mid-air — camera does a 360 orbit snap around the frozen figure. Smash cut: the full Adletic Agency circular logo crashes into frame from above, landing with weight and a dust-puff settling effect. Logo pulses once, holds. Orange and navy blue on white. Aggressive, bold, high-energy. 35mm wide angle lens distortion on close shots. No CGI look.

## Negative Prompt (if supported)
slow motion, floaty, soft, blurry, 3D render, CGI, cartoon, anime, plastic, smooth skin, digital art, watermark, text overlay, low quality, static, boring, gentle

## Recommended Settings
- Duration: 10s
- Motion strength: High (80-100%)
- CFG scale: 5-7 (model dependent)
- Use the runner mascot image as input for img2vid workflows
- Use the full logo image as the final frame / end card reference

## Usage
1. Pick the prompt that matches your workflow (img2vid vs text2vid)
2. Swap in the correct reference image
3. Adjust motion keywords for your specific model
4. This is your universal intro — use before every video
