# Skill: Ad Copy Generator

Write high-converting ad copy for Meta Ads, Google Ads, or organic posts promoting the AI Video Creators Community or client projects.

## Input
Ask the user for:
- **What are we promoting?** — Community, course, service, or client product
- **Target audience** — Creators, freelancers, business owners, etc.
- **Platform** — Meta (FB/IG), Google, WhatsApp, organic post
- **Angle** — Pain point, aspiration, FOMO, social proof, curiosity
- **Offer** — Pricing, discount, free trial, waitlist

## Process
1. Write 3 headline variations
2. Write primary text (short + long version)
3. Write a CTA line
4. If Meta ad: suggest ad format (single image, carousel, video)
5. Use Malaysian market language where appropriate (RM pricing, local references)

## Output
Save to `outputs/ads/` with descriptive filename.

## Rules
- Lead with the hook, not the brand
- One idea per ad — don't cram
- Use numbers and specifics (RM18/mo, 50 spots, 18 lessons)
- Write like a human, not a marketer
