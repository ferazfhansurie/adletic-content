# Skill: Hook Writer

Generate attention-grabbing hooks for short-form video content.

## Input
Ask the user for:
- **Topic** — What is the video about?
- **Audience** — Who's watching?
- **Style** — Controversial, curiosity, shock, relatable, educational

## Process
Generate 10 hooks using these proven frameworks:
1. **Bold claim** — "This AI tool replaced my entire video team"
2. **Question** — "Why are AI creators charging RM65K per project?"
3. **Pattern interrupt** — "Stop scrolling. This will change how you make content."
4. **Curiosity gap** — "I found an AI tool nobody's talking about..."
5. **Social proof** — "This is how I made RM12K from one AI video"
6. **Contrarian** — "Forget Canva. Here's what pros actually use."
7. **Tutorial** — "How to make a cinematic AI video in 60 seconds"
8. **FOMO** — "Only 1% of creators know this AI workflow"
9. **Before/after** — "My videos before AI vs after AI"
10. **Relatable pain** — "POV: You spent 6 hours editing a 15-second video"

## Output
Save to `outputs/hooks/` with descriptive filename.

## Rules
- First 3 words matter most
- Under 10 words if possible
- No clickbait that doesn't deliver
